# The Wynwood Walls Shop – Digital Marketing Strategy & Plan (Working Copy)

This document will be built in stages. Stage 1 below prioritizes parallel execution for Q4 (BFCM window) while laying foundations for scale. References: @Research Files/wynwood_walls_digital_strategy_gaps.md, @Research Files/Holistic Digital Marketing Plan for The Wynwood Walls Shop.md, @data/shopify_analytics_snapshot.md, @data/ga4_summary_last30days.md.

## 0) Objectives and Guardrails (Q4 focus)

- **Revenue acceleration for holidays** while fixing measurement and feed hygiene.
- **Expand reach via marketplaces** (Etsy, Amazon, eBay) using automated product feeds; no manual duplication.
- **Leverage parent brand and site** to route qualified traffic: cross-link artist pages → shop collections; systematic UTMs.
- **Position fashion as wearable art**; begin brand storytelling and content capture.

## 1) KPIs (initial Q4 targets)

- Site conversion rate to 1.0–1.2% (+20–40% from current).
- AOV to $115+ (via upsells/bundles once enabled).
- Ads “true ROAS” ≥ 350% with only Purchase as primary conversion.
- Email revenue share ≥ 7-10% during BFCM.
- Social: +30% shop IG follower growth; weekly reach via parent IG account.
- Marketplaces: 400–600 SKUs live, first $20–30K GMV by end of season.

## 2) Stage 1 Workstreams (run in parallel)

### A) Measurement & Feed Hygiene (Week 0–1)

- Rebuild GTM/GA4 events: `view_item`, `add_to_cart`, `begin_checkout`, `purchase` with value; make Purchase the only primary goal in Ads.
- UTM conventions and channel mapping doc; enable cross-domain attribution between museum site and shop.
- Merchant Center: fix availability/price sync; shipping/tax rules; suppress out-of-stock; title/attribute improvements for Shopping.
- Deliverables: tagging playbook, validated test purchases, Data Studio dashboard stub.

### B) Marketplaces via Data Feeds (Week 0–3)

- Tooling: use existing connectors where possible (CedCommerce/Etsy/eBay/Amazon) or a centralized feed tool if needed.
- Common setup: category/attribute mapping, images, policies, shipping profiles, returns, brand voice, SEO titles.
- Amazon: list allowable categories (books, apparel/collectibles as permitted); confirm brand/rights; pricing and FBA vs FBM decision per SKU.
- Etsy: launch artist-focused storefront; prioritize handmade/collab SKUs; enable Etsy Ads at low daily cap for discovery.
- eBay: open store; listing templates; Best Offer rules for collectibles; shipping matrix.
- Operations: daily feed pushes, exception report for errors, SLA to delist OOS items within 2 hours.
- Deliverables: three storefronts live, 400–600 SKUs listed, marketplace SOP and error log.

### C) Paid Media Reset + BFCM Sprint (Week 0–4)

- Google Ads: pause non-purchase goal values; maintain Shopping/PMax for coverage; add product-set campaigns for top collections; budget consolidation to profitable programs.
- Creative: BFCM offer ladder (free shipping threshold, tiered % off, bundle savings); dynamic product ads.
- Meta: convert to catalog sales + retargeting; creator/UGC hooks around artists and drops. Test multiple ads per item/collection to understand which performs best. Scale up performing ads.
- Deliverables: campaign architecture diagram, audiences, offer sheet, daily pacing dashboard.

### D) Social Leverage from Parent Brand (Week 0–4)

- Weekly cross-post plan: parent IG (125K) → shop IG (6.5K); story reposts, product tags, “tap to shop” (with UTM); consistent Miami location tag.
- On-site synergy: parent website artist pages embed shop product modules with UTMs; link to artist collections to split ranking and pass internal equity.
- Influencers/creators: shortlist local art/fashion creators; whitelisting plan; UGC guidelines.
- Deliverables: 4-week content calendar, asset checklist, UTM map, cross-post cadence.

### E) Live Shopping Pilot (Week 2–4)

- Format: 30–45 min weekly stream from studio; host + guest (artist/curator); 6–10 featured SKUs; limited signed items/giveaways.
- Run-of-show, inventory holds, promo codes, pinned links, stream highlights chopped into Reels/TikTok/Shorts.
- Deliverables: ROS template, tech checklist, promo plan, post-stream edit workflow.

### F) Content & SEO for Holidays (Week 0–3)

- Gift guides: “Street Art Gifts under $50/$100”, “Artist Edition Books”, “Wearable Art”.
- Collection and product SEO refresh for top entry pages; internal links from parent site where relevant.
- Deliverables: 6–8 pages/posts, internal linking map, indexation check.

### G) Email/CRM (Week 0–3)

- Mailchimp: implement welcome, abandon cart, post-purchase, win-back; segment recent engagers to control cost.
- Evaluate Klaviyo migration plan post-holiday for Shopify-native triggers.
- Deliverables: 4 flows live, holiday send calendar, revenue attribution report.

### H) Fashion Category Accelerator (foundation in Stage 1; scale in Stage 2)

- Narrative: “Wearable Art” positioning; artist stories; lookbook and short-form video.
- BFCM capsule: limited units + timed drops; PR and creator seeding.
- Deliverables: brand brief, capsule plan, wholesale prospect list (for Stage 2).

## 3) Fast Timeline (Weeks 0–4)

- Week 0–1: Tagging/feeds fixed; Google Ads hygiene; Etsy/eBay/Amazon storefronts provisioned; content calendar and BFCM offers locked.
- Week 2: First marketplace SKUs live; cross-post cadence begins; first live shopping pilot scheduled.

## 7) Stage 2 — Scale and Systemize (Weeks 5–12)

### Goals & KPIs (incremental)

- Marketplaces GMV: additional $50–$75K with 1,000–1,400 SKUs live; error rate <2%.
- Live shopping: weekly show with $3–5K GMV average; opt-in rate ≥ 5% from viewers.
- Paid media: true ROAS ≥ 300% at scaled budgets; CPA within target by category.
- Email: ≥ 15% of monthly revenue; unsubscribe rate < 0.3% per send.
- Organic: +20% non-branded traffic; top artist collection pages ranking on page 1–2.

### A) Marketplaces at Scale (Automation & Optimization)

- Title/attribute templates by channel; rules-based mapping for variants, materials, artists.
- Repricing/offer logic for eBay (Best Offer, min margins) and Amazon (MAP compliance).
- Ratings/reviews acquisition on Amazon where permitted; post-purchase messaging SOP.
- Bundles/kits for apparel + accessories; marketplace-specific shipping SLAs.
- Health monitoring: nightly job producing “feed errors, OOS, policy” report to Slack/email.
- Deliverables: 1,000–1,400 SKUs live; rules docs; monitoring dashboard; SOP updates.

### B) Live Shopping Program (From Pilot → Program)

- Platform mix: IG Live + YouTube + TikTok (restream), with shoppable links via Link-in-Bio/Shop app; explore TikTok Shop eligibility.
- Show cadence: weekly; rotating themes (artist focus, gift picks, wearable art looks).
- Production: host prep deck, B‑roll inserts, lower thirds; guest scheduling framework.
- Commerce ops: pre-hold inventory, limited signed runs, drop countdowns, dynamic promo codes.
- Post-production: cut 6–10 highlights per show; distribute to Reels/TikTok/Shorts; pin to artist pages.
- Measurement: viewer count, watch time, CTR, orders, GMV, opt-ins; report per episode.
- Deliverables: season calendar (8–10 episodes), kit list, show template, measurement sheet.

### C) Fashion Brand Build (Wearable Art) + Wholesale Pilot

- Positioning: brand brief, lookbook, size charts, care guidance, model/content guidelines.
- Creator program: micro-creators (Miami + art/fashion) with structured seeding and whitelisting.
- Capsule drops: limited units, serialized cards, signed tags; PR pitch list for culture/fashion.
- Wholesale pilot: identify 10–20 boutiques; line sheet, margins, order minimums, ship windows.
- Deliverables: brand system, lookbook shoot, capsule calendar, wholesale prospect pack.

### D) Cross-Site Artist Product Modules — Implementation Spec

- Objective: surface 4–8 relevant products on museum site artist pages and route users to shop.
- Input: artist slug/ID on parent site maps to Shopify `vendor` or `artist` tag.
- Data source: Shopify Storefront GraphQL API; filter `availableForSale = true`.
- Ranking: pinned tag > top-sellers (30d) > newest; fallback to artist collection page if <4 results.
- UI: image, title, price/compare-at, quick badges (Limited/New), CTA “View on Shop”.
- Links: deep link to product on shop with UTMs `utm_source=parent&utm_medium=artist_module&utm_campaign=<artist>`.
- SEO: render as JS/SSR component with `nofollow` on product thumbnails; canonical remains product page; component schema as ItemList optional.
- Performance: server-side cache (TTL 15 min); image srcset; component weight <120KB.
- Tracking: GA4 `select_content`/custom `artist_module_click` with artist/product IDs on parent site; purchase attribution via UTMs in shop GA4/Ads.
- Admin controls: allow manual pinning/exclusion via Shopify metafields/tags.
- Deliverables: tech spec, component (parent site), metafield schema (shop), UAT, launch checklist.

### E) Paid Media Scale-Up

## 8) UTM & Attribution Standards (Immediate Adoption)

- `utm_source`: channel or site, e.g., google, meta, parent_site, email, etsy, ebay, amazon.
- `utm_medium`: cpc, social, referral, email, live, artist_module.
- `utm_campaign`: collection/artist or promotion, e.g., bfcm_2025_books, wearable_art_capsule, live_ep01.
- `utm_content`: ad or creative variant, e.g., video_a, static_b, car1, story_tag.
- `utm_term`: keyword when applicable (search only).
- Examples:
  - Parent artist module → product: `utm_source=parent_site&utm_medium=artist_module&utm_campaign=<artist_slug>&utm_content=slot1`
  - IG story link (parent → shop): `utm_source=meta&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=bfcm_2025_capsule&utm_content=story_repost`
  - Live shopping landing: `utm_source=live&utm_medium=video&utm_campaign=live_ep01&utm_content=promo_pin`
  - Marketplace promoted listing: `utm_source=etsy&utm_medium=cpc&utm_campaign=gifts_under_50&utm_content=auto_ad`

Governance:

- Single source of truth sheet for campaigns and values.
- Enforce lowercase, underscores, no spaces.
- GA4 channel grouping map updated to recognize the above.

## 11) Stage 3 — Always‑On Growth (Weeks 13–24)

### Goals & KPIs (retention and lifetime value)

- Repeat purchase rate +50% vs. current 6‑month baseline.
- 90‑day LTV +25%; subscription program 300–500 active members; churn <7%/mo.
- Reviews: +2,000 net product reviews; UGC: 10+ high‑quality posts/week.
- Email share 20–25% of revenue with stable deliverability (<0.3% unsubscribe).
- Paid media creative refresh every 21–28 days; CTR +20%, CPA within guardrails.

### A) Loyalty & Membership

- Evaluate Shopify loyalty apps (e.g., Smile, LoyaltyLion) for points, tiers, and VIP early access.
- Benefits: free shipping thresholds, exclusive drops, double‑points windows, birthday rewards.
- Integrations: Klaviyo (post‑holiday) for tier‑based messaging; onsite account widget; POS linkage.
- Deliverables: program rules, assets, onsite widget, email/SMS sequences, KPI report.

### B) Subscription & Drops Engine

- “Wearable Art Club” monthly bundle (tee/hat/accessory) or quarterly book bundle.
- Ops: pre‑order window, inventory holds, packaging, billing retries, skip/pause UX.
- Content: unboxings with creators; behind‑the‑scenes of design/printing.
- Metrics: CAC vs. LTV, retention cohorts, add‑on attach rate.

### C) CRM & Lifecycle Expansion (post‑holiday migration to Klaviyo, if chosen)

- Predictive segments (churn risk, next best product), RFM cohorts, artist affinity lists.
- Flows: browse abandon by collection, replenishment/size restock, VIP early‑access, win‑back with artist picks.
- Reviews/Q&A: integrate Judge.me/Okendo; auto‑request with incentives; syndicate to marketplaces where allowed.

### D) Community & Editorial

- Launch YouTube/TikTok series: artist studio visits, process, Miami culture, live show highlights.
- Monthly editorial calendar across parent + shop; reciprocal links; SEO schema for videos.
- Discord/IG Broadcast Channel pilot for drops and behind‑the‑scenes access.

### E) Wholesale & Partnerships (scale from Stage 2 pilot)

- B2B portal (Shopify Plus/wholesale app): price lists, MOQ, case packs, net terms.
- Target 10–20 boutiques; PR + lookbook mailers; co‑op content kits for retailers.
- Metrics: wholesale GMV, reorder rate, door survival after 90 days.

### F) Analytics Maturity

- Dashboard v2: cohort LTV, CAC by channel, contribution margin by category, marketplace P&L.
- MMM‑lite: budget reallocation rules using lagged ROAS and contribution margin.
- Data QA cadence: weekly anomaly detection; monthly governance review.

### G) Experimentation & CRO Program

- Backlog: sticky ATC, mobile PDP layout, image sequencing, trust blocks, shipping estimator, free‑shipping threshold, upsell engine, recs widgets, search tuning.
- Tools: native theme tests + lightweight A/B platform; heatmaps and session recordings.

### H) Operating Cadence & Governance

- Weekly growth stand‑up (45 min): scorecards, blockers, reallocations.

- Quarterly planning: capsule calendar, creator roster, wholesale pipeline, budget.
